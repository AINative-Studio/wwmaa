## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used to make websites work efficiently and provide information to website owners.

Cookies can be "session" cookies (temporary and deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent" cookies (remain on your device until deleted or expired).

## Types of Cookies We Use

We categorize cookies into four types, aligned with GDPR and CCPA requirements:

### 1. Essential Cookies (Always Active)

**Purpose:** These cookies are necessary for the Website to function properly and cannot be disabled.

**What they do:**
- Enable basic site functionality like page navigation
- Maintain security and prevent fraud
- Remember your cookie consent preferences
- Maintain your login session

**Legal Basis:** Legitimate interest (necessary for site functionality)

**Examples:**
- `user_cookie_consent` - Stores your cookie preferences
- Session management cookies
- Security tokens

**Retention:** Session or up to 12 months

### 2. Functional Cookies

**Purpose:** These cookies enhance functionality and personalization but are not essential.

**What they do:**
- Remember your preferences (language, region, theme)
- Remember your login state across sessions
- Enable enhanced features like video players
- Auto-fill forms with your saved information

**Legal Basis:** Consent (you can opt-in or opt-out)

**Examples:**
- Language preference cookies
- Theme/display preference cookies
- User interface customization

**Retention:** Up to 12 months

**How to control:** You can disable these cookies in our Cookie Preferences modal without affecting essential site functionality.

### 3. Analytics Cookies

**Purpose:** These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our Website.

**What they do:**
- Collect anonymous information about page visits
- Track user behavior and navigation patterns
- Measure website performance and errors
- Identify popular content and pages
- Help us improve our Website based on usage data

**Legal Basis:** Consent (you can opt-in or opt-out)

**Third-Party Services:**
- **Google Analytics 4** - We use GA4 to analyze website traffic and user behavior. Google may use this data in accordance with their privacy policy. [Learn more about Google Analytics](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)

**Data Collected:**
- Pages visited
- Time spent on site
- Referring website/source
- Browser and device information (anonymized)
- Approximate location (city/country level)

**IP Anonymization:** We enable IP anonymization in Google Analytics to enhance privacy.

**Retention:** Up to 26 months (Google Analytics default)

**How to control:** You can disable these cookies in our Cookie Preferences modal. You can also use the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

### 4. Marketing Cookies

**Purpose:** These cookies track your online activity to deliver more relevant advertising.

**What they do:**
- Track your browsing behavior across websites
- Build a profile of your interests
- Deliver targeted advertisements
- Measure advertising campaign effectiveness
- Enable retargeting campaigns

**Legal Basis:** Consent (you can opt-in or opt-out)

**Third-Party Services:**
Currently, we do not use marketing cookies. If we implement them in the future, we will:
- Update this policy
- Obtain your explicit consent
- Provide clear information about third-party advertisers

**Retention:** Varies by provider (typically 30-90 days)

**How to control:** You can disable these cookies in our Cookie Preferences modal.

## Your Cookie Choices

### Managing Cookie Preferences

You have several options to control cookies:

#### 1. Cookie Preferences Modal

Click "Cookie Preferences" in our website footer to:
- View your current cookie settings
- Enable or disable each cookie category
- Accept all cookies
- Reject all non-essential cookies
- Save custom preferences

Your preferences are stored for 12 months and will be remembered on future visits.

#### 2. Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to:
- View and delete cookies
- Block cookies from specific sites
- Block all cookies
- Delete cookies when you close the browser

**Browser Cookie Settings:**
- [Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)
- [Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/cookies-information-websites-store-on-your-computer)
- [Safari](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-and-website-data-sfri11471/mac)
- [Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge-63947406-40ac-c3b8-57b9-2a946a29ae09)

**Note:** Blocking all cookies may prevent some parts of our Website from functioning properly.

#### 3. Third-Party Opt-Outs

For analytics cookies:
- [Google Analytics Opt-out](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)

For advertising cookies (if applicable):
- [Network Advertising Initiative Opt-out](http://optout.networkadvertising.org/)
- [Digital Advertising Alliance Opt-out](http://optout.aboutads.info/)

### Do Not Track (DNT)

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal. Currently, there is no industry standard for how to respond to DNT signals. We do not currently respond to DNT signals, but we respect your cookie preferences set via our Cookie Preferences modal.
